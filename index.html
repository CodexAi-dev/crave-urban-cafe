The Crave Experience

More than a café —
a state of mind

Nestled in the heart of Negombo, Crave – The Urban Café is a sanctuary where lush tropical greenery meets sophisticated urban energy. We craft spaces that invite you to linger, connect, and savour every moment. Our carefully curated menu celebrates local flavours while embracing global culinary traditions, ensuring that every visit is a journey of discovery.

Whether you're catching up with friends over cold brew, working through the afternoon with our signature flat white, or celebrating a special occasion — Crave is your home away from home. We believe in creating an atmosphere where conversations flow freely, creativity blooms, and memories are made over exceptional food and drink.
